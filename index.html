About Me

I’m an Embedded Systems Engineer with 8+ years of experience architecting and developing real-time, secure, and scalable firmware for IoT devices and industrial systems. I specialize in STM32, Renesas RA, PIC32, Zephyr, and FreeRTOS. My expertise includes secure OTA, bootloaders, BLE, LoRa, and MQTT with strong compliance to MISRA C and OWASP IoT standards. I’ve also developed automation tools in C#/Qt and worked across the full product lifecycle — from firmware to cloud to web integration.
